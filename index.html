FAQ

How long is the program?
Our intensive program lasts 12 weeks, with 20+ hours of content per week.
Do I need prior experience?
Basic design knowledge is recommended but not required. We start from fundamentals.
What software do I need?
Adobe Creative Suite (Illustrator, Photoshop, After Effects) and Figma.
Is there job placement?
Yes! Our Pro and Intensive programs include job placement assistance.
Can I work while studying?
Yes, our program is designed for both full-time students and working professionals.
